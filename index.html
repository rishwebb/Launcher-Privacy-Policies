Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coveil - Minimal Launcher ("Coveil", "the app", "we") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is straightforward: Coveil does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Coveil does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account registration, login, or any form of identity verification. No data is collected from your device and transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party.

2. Permissions

Coveil requests only the permissions necessary to function as a home screen launcher on Android. These permissions are used solely on-device and are never used to collect or transmit data. The permissions used may include:

  • Query installed packages - Required to display the list of installed apps on your device within the launcher.
  • Set as default launcher - Required to function as your home screen.
  • Receive boot completed - Required to restore Coveil as the active launcher after a device restart.

No permissions related to contacts, messages, camera, microphone, location, or external storage are requested.

3. Data Storage

Any preferences or settings you configure within Coveil, such as hidden apps or layout preferences, are stored locally on your device using Android's standard shared preferences storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Coveil does not integrate any third-party analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party libraries that collect user data. There are no advertisements of any kind within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Coveil does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, the app is safe for use by all age groups.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy in the future, the updated policy will be published on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Open Source

Coveil is developed independently. We believe in transparency. If you have questions about how the app works technically, you are welcome to contact us directly.
